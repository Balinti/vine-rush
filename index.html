How to Play Vine Rush

Auto-run through the jungle corridor. Tap or press Space to latch onto a vine anchor and swing through the gap in the thorn wall. Land clean for a Clean Cut bonus!

Controls

  • Tap / Click — latch onto vine anchor
  • Space or Enter — latch (keyboard)

Tips

  • Wait for the anchor glow to peak before tapping — perfect timing earns combo points.
  • Gates get narrower as your score climbs. Stay focused!
  • Decoy anchors (red outline) appear after score 50 — avoid them or get a weak swing.
  • Near-misses reward sparks; direct hits end your run.
FAQ

Is Vine Rush free?

Yes — completely free, no download required. Play directly in your browser.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes — optimized for touch on iOS and Android.

How is the high score saved?

Your best score is stored locally in your browser using localStorage.
